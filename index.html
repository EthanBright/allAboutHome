About Us

Proper maintenance is critical to maintaining a home's value. Our pre-scheduled services keep your home in great shape throughout the year. As part of our monthly house visits, we perform seasonal tasks such as gutter cleaning and critical annual services to ensure you'll be ready for the hottest months or the coldest ones. We offer many home repair services and have a network of personally trusted subcontractors when needed.

How it works

1. Assessment: A free home maintenance assessment is the first step. We can understand your priorities for maintenance and any particular repair needs around your property by doing so.

2. Proposal: Within a couple of days after the walkthrough, we'll send you a detailed proposal detailing the monthly price and services included. You can customize your plan, adding or removing services as you see fit so that you only pay for what you need to keep your house in top shape.

3. Contract: We present a simple contract outlining our services and the agreed-upon price following the service schedule. You can terminate the agreement at any time. Your services will be performed on a typical day of the month once we have signed the contract.

It's that simple! Rest easy knowing that you're doing a great job of taking care of your home.
